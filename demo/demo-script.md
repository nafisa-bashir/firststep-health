# Demo Script

## FirstStep Health

**Estimated Duration:** 2 to 3 minutes

---

## 1. Introduction

FirstStep Health is a Rural Healthcare Business Launch Assistant designed to help aspiring healthcare entrepreneurs in Al Qua'a, Abu Dhabi understand the official steps involved in starting a healthcare related business.

Rather than searching across multiple government websites, users answer a few questions and receive a personalized roadmap based on verified UAE resources.

---

## 2. Language Selection

Begin by showing the language selection screen.

Explain that the application supports both English and Arabic to improve accessibility for a wider range of users.

---

## 3. Questionnaire

Complete the questionnaire.

Explain that the roadmap is generated using:

- Intended healthcare service
- Existing qualification
- Available budget
- Preferred operating model
- Intended location

---

## 4. Generate Roadmap

Click **Generate My Roadmap**.

Explain that the application creates a personalized roadmap based on the user's responses.

---

## 5. Review the Roadmap

Briefly explain each section:

- Personalized introduction
- Recommended first step
- Why this roadmap
- Things to verify
- Possible approval pathway
- Required documents
- Funding opportunities
- Estimated startup costs
- Suggested timeline
- Immediate next steps
- Official UAE resources
- Disclaimer

---

## 6. Official Sources

Open one of the official links.

Explain that FirstStep Health does not replace government authorities and encourages users to verify every recommendation directly with the appropriate organization.

---

## 7. Closing

Conclude by explaining that FirstStep Health simplifies the early planning process for aspiring healthcare entrepreneurs while encouraging responsible use of official government resources.

Thank you.

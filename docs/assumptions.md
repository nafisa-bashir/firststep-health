# Assumptions

## Overview

FirstStep Health is a planning assistant designed to help aspiring healthcare entrepreneurs understand the possible steps involved in starting a healthcare related business in Abu Dhabi.

The project is based on the following assumptions.

---

# User Assumptions

The application assumes that users:

- Already possess healthcare related knowledge, qualifications, or experience.
- Intend to establish a legal and licensed healthcare related business.
- Have access to the internet and can use a web application.
- Are seeking guidance during the early planning stage of starting a business.

FirstStep Health does not train healthcare professionals or verify professional qualifications.

---

# Geographic Assumptions

This prototype is designed specifically for:

- Al Qua'a
- Abu Dhabi Emirate
- United Arab Emirates

Licensing authorities, funding programmes, and regulatory guidance referenced within the application are specific to Abu Dhabi.

Entrepreneurs operating in other Emirates may need to follow different regulatory pathways.

---

# Regulatory Assumptions

The application assumes that:

- Government licensing procedures may change over time.
- Fees, documentation requirements, and approval processes are subject to updates by the relevant authorities.
- Different healthcare services may require different licensing pathways.

For this reason, every recommendation generated by the application should be treated as a possible pathway rather than a guaranteed process.

Users are encouraged to verify all information directly with the appropriate government authority before taking action.

---

# Funding Assumptions

Funding opportunities presented within the application are based on publicly available information.

The application assumes that:

- Eligibility criteria may change.
- Not every user will qualify for every funding programme.
- Funding amounts are indicative rather than guaranteed.

Users should always confirm the latest requirements directly with the funding organization before applying.

---

# Cost Assumptions

Estimated startup costs are intended as planning guidance only.

Actual costs may vary depending on factors such as:

- Business activity
- Equipment requirements
- Legal structure
- Government fee changes
- Professional service costs

Where official government fees are publicly available, these are referenced separately.

---

# Scope Assumptions

FirstStep Health is designed to support business planning only.

The application does not:

- provide legal advice
- provide medical advice
- issue business licenses
- approve healthcare services
- guarantee funding approval
- replace government licensing authorities

Instead, it helps users organize publicly available information into a structured roadmap before beginning the official application process.

---

# Future Considerations

As the project evolves, these assumptions should be reviewed and updated to reflect:

- Changes in government regulations.
- Additional healthcare service categories.
- Expansion to other Emirates.
- New funding programmes.
- Feedback collected from future users.

Maintaining accurate assumptions will help ensure the application continues to provide responsible and trustworthy guidance.

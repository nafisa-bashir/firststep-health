# Research Categories

The collected sources were used across five main areas.

## Problem Evidence

Evidence supporting the existence of rural healthcare accessibility challenges and the need for clearer entrepreneurial guidance.

---

## Licensing and Regulation

Official government publications used to understand healthcare licensing, business registration, and regulatory pathways.

---

## Funding

Government and public funding programmes available to entrepreneurs, including eligibility guidance and published funding schemes.

---

## Market Context

Published market reports used to understand trends within the UAE healthcare sector and the continued demand for community healthcare services.

---

## National Strategy

Government strategies and policy documents demonstrating alignment with broader UAE healthcare and entrepreneurship objectives.

---

# Research Principles

During development, the following principles were followed:

- Preference was given to official UAE government sources whenever available.
- Academic literature was used to support claims about the underlying problem.
- Market reports were used only to provide industry context.
- Regulatory information was presented as a possible pathway rather than legal advice.
- Funding information reflects publicly available guidance and does not guarantee eligibility or approval.

---

# Disclaimer

Government regulations, funding programmes, and licensing requirements may change over time.

Users should always verify the latest information directly with the relevant authority before making business or regulatory decisions.
